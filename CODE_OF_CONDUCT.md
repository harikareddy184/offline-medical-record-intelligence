# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project
environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing harm directly.
- Respect privacy, especially around medical data.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and sharing private medical data
without consent are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict access when
behavior violates this code.
